# App Metadata Guidelines

App metadata refers to the textual information submitted to Apple App Store Connect and the Google Play Console. This text is critical for App Store Optimization (ASO) and user conversion.

## Common Metadata

### App Name / Title

- **Purpose**: The most important keyword ranking factor.
- **Apple App Store**: Max 30 characters. E.g., "Parable Bloom: [Sub-brand/Feature]".
- **Google Play Store**: Max 30 characters.

### Long Description

- **Purpose**: Describes the app's features in detail.
- **Apple App Store**: Max 4000 characters. Not heavily indexed for search keywords, but critical for user conversion.
- **Google Play Store**: Max 4000 characters. **Heavily indexed** for search. Repeat key search terms naturally 3-5 times throughout the text.

---

## Apple App Store Specifics

### Subtitle

- **Length**: 30 characters max.
- **Purpose**: Appears under the app name. Highly indexed for keywords. Use descriptive action words.

### Keywords Field

- **Length**: 100 characters max.
- **Purpose**: The primary way Apple indexes the app.
- **Rules**:
  - Separate words with commas (no spaces after commas: `word,another,thing`).
  - Do not use plural forms if singular is included.
  - Do not include the app name or category name (they are automatically indexed).

### Promotional Text

- **Length**: 170 characters max.
- **Purpose**: Appears at the top of the description. This is the **only** text field that can be updated without submitting a new app build to review. Useful for temporary promotions or announcements.

---

## Google Play Store Specifics

### Short Description

- **Length**: 80 characters max.
- **Purpose**: Appears above the "Read More" button. Highly indexed for keywords and critical for conversion.

### Keyword Strategy

- Google does not have a dedicated "Keywords" field. It scrapes the Title, Short Description, and Long Description for keywords. Ensure core ASO keywords are strategically placed in these fields.

---

## Localization

- **Current Strategy**: English only.
- **Future Consideration**: Both platforms allow localizing metadata. Translating the Title, Subtitle/Short Description, and Keywords into other languages can dramatically increase global reach.
