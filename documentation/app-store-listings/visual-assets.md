# Visual Assets Guidelines

This document details the visual requirements for the App Store and Google Play listings.

## App Icon

- **Apple App Store**:
  - Size: 1024 x 1024 pixels.
  - Format: PNG (no transparency or alpha channels).
  - Apple automatically applies rounded corners, so the uploaded image must be square.
- **Google Play Store**:
  - Size: 512 x 512 pixels.
  - Format: 32-bit PNG (with alpha).
  - Google Play uses "Adaptive Icons" which mask the icon into various shapes (circles, squarcles, etc.). Keep the core logo in the center "safe zone".

## Screenshots

Screenshots are arguably the most important conversion asset. You should treat them like a billboard or a presentation rather than literal unedited screenshots. Use a frame, add text captions at the top or bottom, and highlight the value proposition.

### Apple App Store

- **Required Sizes**:
  - **6.5-inch** (iPhone 14 Plus, 13 Pro Max, etc.): 1284 x 2778 pixels
  - **5.5-inch** (iPhone 8 Plus, etc. - Required if supporting older devices): 1242 x 2208 pixels
  - **12.9-inch iPad** (Required if supporting iPad): 2048 x 2732 pixels
- **Quantity**: Up to 10 screenshots.
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG.

### Google Play Store

- **Required Sizes**:
  - Phones: Minimum dimension 320px, maximum dimension 3840px. (Aspect ratio 16:9 or 9:16 recommended).
  - Tablets: If the app supports tablets, upload 7-inch and 10-inch specific screenshots.
- **Quantity**: 2 to 8 screenshots per form factor.

## Feature Graphic (Google Play Only)

- **Size**: 1024 x 500 pixels.
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG.
- **Purpose**: This is mandatory for Google Play. It appears at the top of your store listing and in various promotional spots. Do not put text or critical visuals near the edges or in the bottom right corner (where the video play button goes).

## App Preview Videos

- **Apple App Store (App Preview)**:
  - Max duration: 30 seconds.
  - Must primarily show in-app UI. Apple rejects videos that feel like "commercials" with too much live-action footage or out-of-app animation.
  - Muted by default; text overlays are essential.
- **Google Play Store (Promo Video)**:
  - Provided via a YouTube URL.
  - Can be more cinematic and commercial-like than Apple's.
  - Must not be monetized (no ads on the YouTube video). Turn off YouTube ads for that specific video.
