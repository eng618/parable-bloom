# Marketing & Launch Materials

Preparing for a launch involves coordinating materials beyond just what is uploaded to the store consoles.

## Release Notes ("What's New")

When pushing an update, you must provide release notes.

- **Apple App Store**: Required for every update. Text is added in the "What's New in This Version" section.
- **Google Play Store**: Added as "Release Notes" bounded by `<en-US>` tags (or other locales).

**Best Practice for Release Notes:**
Don't just write "Bug fixes and performance improvements." Take the opportunity to engage users, highlight new features, and show the personality of Parable Bloom.

## Website & Landing Page

The primary website is hosted at: `https://parable-bloom.pages.dev/`

**Action Items for Launch:**

- Ensure the site has clear "Download on the App Store" and "Get it on Google Play" badges pointing to the respective store listings.
- Include a section for Support/Troubleshooting (required by App Store).

## Press Kit (Optional but Recommended)

A press kit helps journalists and reviewers write about Parable Bloom. It should ideally be a downloadable `.zip` file or a Google Drive link hosted on the website.
It should contain:

- High-res app icons (with and without backgrounds).
- Lifestyle images or promotional device renders.
- Clean screenshots.
- A one-pager PDF describing the app, its origin story, target audience, and key features.
- Contact information.
